Celebrant of the Day

Septa's legend is a year older today

Give it up for the one and only Jennifer Adeyinka! Our Project Manager in the trenches is a rockstar who casually turns chaos into beautifully organized sprints.

When she is not saving our project timelines, Jennifer is the undisputed mastermind behind the legendary office “Best Dressed Friday” competition and is always the first to bring in her world-famous home-made Efo Riro, bringing the laughs and good vibes daily.

So ditch your keyboards, grab a slice of cake, and help us celebrate the human who makes coming to work feel like a highly functional, slightly ridiculous party.

Happy Birthday Jennifer,

You deserve it!
